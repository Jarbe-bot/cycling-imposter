Cycling Imposter - The Daily Cycling Game

Welcome to Cycling Imposter, the ultimate daily cycling trivia game. Every day, test your knowledge of the pro peloton. We present you with a grid of 8 professional cyclists, but one of them doesn't fit the daily statement. Can you find the fake rider? It is the perfect daily puzzle for fans of the Tour de France, Giro d'Italia, Vuelta a España, and the Classics.
